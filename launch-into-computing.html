Launch into Computing

Module Overview

This module introduces core computing concepts and provides a foundation for further study in artificial intelligence. It covers areas such as logic, software engineering, data science, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and enterprise IT management.

Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and critically analyse computing challenges and processes in business systems.
  • Gather and synthesise information from multiple sources.
  • Evaluate methodologies, tools, and techniques to solve computing issues.
  • Understand legal, social, ethical, and professional issues in computing.
